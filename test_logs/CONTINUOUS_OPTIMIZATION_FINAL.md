## 📝 日志文件使用

**运行测试并保存日志**:
```powershell

```

**分析日志**:
```powershell
.\analyze_risk_test_log.ps1
```

**查看摘要**:
```powershell
Get-Content test_logs/pytest_risk_latest.log | Select-String -Pattern "(passed|failed|skipped|error|===)" | Select-Object -Last 3
```
